Amer fort-Jaipur

Amer fort, also known as amber fort, is one of the most famous historical
        monuments in jaipur, Rajasthan.

History

      It was built in 1592 by Raja Man Singh
     and is known for its beautiful rajput architecture,large gates, courtyyards, and artistic carving.
